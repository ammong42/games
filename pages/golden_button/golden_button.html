How to Play

At the start of your turn, you automatically gain chips equal to your Draw Count. You may spend chips to do the following actions any number of times, and you cannot end your turn while holding more chips than your Hand Limit.

  • Spend 1 chip to press the Golden Button and roll for an instant win.
  • Spend 2 chips to permanently increase your Hand Limit by 1.
  • Spend 3 chips to permanently increase your Draw Count by 1.
